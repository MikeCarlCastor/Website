Introduction

A. Overview of the Power BI Project

This Power BI project serves as a platform to showcase my skills in data analysis and visualization using Power BI. Leveraging the provided sales dataset, I will demonstrate my proficiency in transforming raw data into meaningful insights and visually appealing dashboards.

The dataset contains valuable sales information, including order IDs, products, quantities ordered, prices, order dates, and purchase addresses. Through the application of Power BI, I will showcase my abilities in extracting insights, identifying trends, and presenting data-driven narratives.

Key focus areas for this project include:

  1. Data Preparation: I will perform data cleansing, transformation, and aggregation to ensure the dataset is ready for analysis. This step will demonstrate my proficiency in data wrangling and preparing clean data for visualization.
  2. Visual Exploration: Using Power BI's interactive visualizations, I will explore the sales data to uncover patterns, trends, and correlations. I will utilize various chart types, such as bar charts, line graphs, and maps, to present the data in an engaging and informative manner.
  3. Sales Performance Analysis: I will analyze sales performance across different dimensions, such as years, months, and products. This analysis will provide insights into revenue trends, top-selling items, and seasonal variations in sales.
  4. Geographic Insights: By leveraging geographic data, I will create maps and visualizations to identify regions with the highest sales and explore potential market opportunities. This analysis will demonstrate my ability to derive actionable insights from spatial data.
  5. Interactive Reports: I will design interactive reports and dashboards that allow users to explore the sales data dynamically. Users will have the flexibility to drill down into specific time periods, products, or regions to gain deeper insights.

B. Project Objectives & Goals

  • Objective 1: Identify the best month for sales and calculate the revenue achieved in each month (January 2019 to December 2019).
  • Objective 2: Determine the city with the highest number of sales and analyze the factors contributing to its success.
  • Objective 3: Optimize advertisement timing by analyzing the data to determine the most effective time for displaying ads that maximize the likelihood of customer purchases.
  • Objective 4: Identify frequently co-purchased products and provide recommendations for cross-selling opportunities.
  • Objective 5: Determine the product that sold the most and provide insights into the factors contributing to its success.
  • Objective 6: Estimate the probabilities of future orders for specific products, such as USB-C Charging Cable, iPhone, Google Phone, and Wired Headphones.
  • Objective 7: Apply forecasting techniques to predict future sales trends and estimate future revenue based on historical data. Evaluate the accuracy of the forecast and refine the forecasting model if necessary.
