From the video above, I observed that there were no potential outliers in the data. Analysis #2. Calculate the average call duration for each product

Based on the analysis of call duration, it was found that Personal Loan has the longest average duration of 18 minutes and 43 seconds, followed by Car Loan with an average duration of 7 minutes and 53 seconds. Analysis #3. Analyze historical call volume patterns to identify peak periods


► Click to show findings

The analysis of weekly call volume reveals that Fridays and Saturdays exhibit the highest call volumes, with 1,008 and 1,031 calls respectively. These Click to show findings indicate that Fridays and Saturdays are the peak periods for call activity, with significantly more calls received compared to other days of the week. Understanding these peak periods is crucial for the company to allocate appropriate resources and ensure efficient handling of customer calls during these high-demand periods.



Analysis #4. Identify factors contributing to longer customer waiting times


► Click to show findings

The analysis reveals a strong correlation between call volume and customer waiting times, particularly during peak periods. As the number of calls an agent receives increases, the customer waiting times also tend to lengthen. This suggests that high call volumes contribute to longer customer waiting times, indicating potential challenges in resource allocation and management.



Analysis #5. Identify factors contributing to low customer satisfaction ratings


► Click to show findings

The analysis highlights a clear correlation between increasing call volume and longer waiting times, which in turn negatively impacts customer satisfaction ratings. This suggests that high call volumes create a bottleneck in customer service, leading to frustration and dissatisfaction among customers. It is important for the company to address this issue by implementing strategies to efficiently manage call volume and reduce waiting times, ultimately enhancing customer satisfaction levels.
