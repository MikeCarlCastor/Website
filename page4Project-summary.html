PROJECT SUMMARY

Ⅰ. Conclusion

Through a comprehensive analysis of the call center dataset, valuable insights have been gained regarding the factors contributing to low customer satisfaction rates. By examining agent performance, call handling metrics, and queue times, correlations and patterns were identified that shed light on the root causes of the problem. These findings have enabled the generation of actionable recommendations to improve customer satisfaction and enhance the overall call center experience.



Ⅱ. Challenges

The project encountered several challenges, with data cleaning and preprocessing being a primary hurdle. Dealing with missing values, inconsistencies, and ensuring data accuracy and consistency required meticulous attention. Furthermore, integrating reference tables into the analysis and merging relevant information with the call center dataset presented complexities, but proved pivotal in obtaining comprehensive insights.



Ⅲ. Key Techniques Employed

During the analysis, several sophisticated techniques were employed to derive meaningful insights:

  1. Statistical analysis: Utilizing statistical methods to quantify relationships, identify trends, and determine the significance of factors affecting customer satisfaction.
  2. Data visualization: Employing charts, graphs, and visual representations to present complex information in an easily understandable format, facilitating the identification of patterns and trends.
  3. Exploratory data analysis: Conducting exploratory techniques to uncover hidden patterns, outliers, and relationships within the dataset, leading to a deeper understanding of the underlying issues.
  4. Automation with VBA and Macros: Leveraging the power of VBA and macros, and with the assistance of ChatGPT, automating descriptive statistics and boxplots to streamline the analysis process and make it more efficient. This approach saved time and effort while allowing for dynamic and customized analyses based on specific requirements.


Ⅳ. Opportunities for Further Analysis

While the analysis conducted in this project provided valuable insights, there are additional steps that could be taken to further enhance the investigation of low customer satisfaction rates. Some potential avenues to explore include:

  1. Sentiment analysis: Utilizing statistical methods to quantify relationships, identify trends, and determine the significance of factors affecting customer satisfaction.
  2. Comparative analysis: Comparing performance metrics, customer satisfaction rates, and other factors across different agents, teams, or products to identify high-performing and low-performing areas for further investigation.
  3. Predictive modeling: Building predictive models to forecast customer satisfaction based on various factors and explore potential interventions to improve satisfaction in real-time.

By considering these additional steps, the analysis can provide a more comprehensive understanding of the factors influencing customer satisfaction, enabling more targeted and effective recommendations for improvement. Continued exploration and evaluation will support ongoing enhancements in customer satisfaction and drive the company's commitment to delivering exceptional customer service.
