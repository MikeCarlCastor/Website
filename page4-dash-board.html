DASHBOARD

OVERVIEW

The Call Center Performance Dashboard is designed to cater to the needs of the Call Center Management Team, Human Resources Department, Quality Assurance Team, and Operations and Workforce Management Team. This comprehensive dashboard provides valuable insights into the call center's performance, enabling these teams to make data-driven decisions and drive operational excellence.

The dashboard offers a range of key metrics and visualizations to assess call center performance. It includes a radar chart displaying call volume versus queue time by products, a pie chart showcasing customer ratings by products, and average holding time by products. These visualizations provide a holistic view of performance across different product lines, empowering teams to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.

A standout feature of the dashboard is the ability to highlight factors contributing to low customer satisfaction ratings. By identifying and analyzing these factors, the Call Center Management Team, Human Resources Department, Quality Assurance Team, and Operations and Workforce Management Team can collaborate to implement targeted strategies and initiatives to enhance customer satisfaction.

Additionally, the dashboard includes summary cards that present key performance indicators such as total calls handled, overall average queue time, overall average customer ratings, and overall average holding time. These cards offer a quick snapshot of overall performance, enabling teams to monitor progress and track performance against set goals.

To facilitate a personalized and focused analysis, the dashboard incorporates interactivity features, including slicers that allow users to filter and analyze data based on specific agents, products, or other relevant dimensions. This interactivity empowers the teams to drill down into specific areas of interest and gain deeper insights.

The Call Center Performance Dashboard serves as a powerful tool for the Call Center Management Team, Human Resources Department, Quality Assurance Team, and Operations and Workforce Management Team. It enables these teams to monitor performance, identify improvement opportunities, and drive data-informed decisions to optimize call center operations and enhance customer satisfaction.
